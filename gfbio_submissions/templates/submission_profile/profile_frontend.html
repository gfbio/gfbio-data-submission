Important Notice: ENA Submission Processing Downtime

The European Nucleotide Archive (ENA) will undergo scheduled cluster downtime and maintenance from Monday, May 12th, to Friday, May 16th, 2025. During this period, ENA will not be processing submissions, and any new submissions may encounter issues during the submission process. Following ENA's advice, no data should be submitted until Friday, 16th May.

If you are planning to submit data to ENA during this time via our Submission Service, be aware that there will be delays in the processing and brokering of your data.

Thank you for your understanding.
